Framework Guidance

Elevate your prompt engineering. Stop asking AI what to do; ask it to be the person who already did it.

  • 1
    Identity Prompting

    Don't ask AI for generic advice. Ask it to respond as the future version of you who successfully solved the exact problem you are facing today.

  • 2
    Constraint Prompting

    Add strict limits like time, budget, energy, and skill level to force the AI out of textbook answers and into highly practical, executable steps.

  • 3
    Reverse Fear Prompting

    Instead of asking how to succeed, ask the AI what specific mistake your future self would urgently warn you about making today.
